Puppy

A puppy is a juvenile dog. All healthy puppies grow quickly after birth. A puppy's coat color may change as the puppy grows older.

  • Kingdom : Animalia
  • Class : Mammalia
  • Order : Carnivora
  • Family : Canidae

Puppies are born with a fully functional sense of smell but can't open their eyes. During their first two weeks, a puppy's senses all develop rapidly.
